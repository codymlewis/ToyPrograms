List of Cuisines

  • Asian: cuisine that emphasises the use opposing flavours. Asia is the largest and most populous continent causing it's cuisine to have immence variations. Commonly used ingredients accross the cultures are rice, ginger, garlic, chillis, sesame seeds, onions, soy and tofu. The foods are typically either stir fried, steamed or deep fried.
  • BBQ & Grill: There are many countries which cook using barbecue and grill the cooking style provides a char flavour to the foods. Such flavours tend to provide a harsher taste to the foods made within this cuisine
  • Burgers: A staple food of American cuisine, albiet being known for use in the USA, there many types of burgers utilizing elements from many other cuisines.
  • Italian: cuisine that is characterized by it's simplicity and regional diversity. The quality of the food is reliant primarilty on the quality of ingredients rather than the preparation. Pasta, vegetables, olive oil and fish are major parts of the cuisine.
  • Thai: A cuisine that emphasises lightly prepared dishes with strong aromas and a spicy edge.
